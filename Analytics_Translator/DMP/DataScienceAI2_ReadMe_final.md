# Research Project README

Welcome to our research project repository. Below you will find a detailed description of the contents of the folder and file in this repository, along with the purpose of each item.


## DMP (Data Management Plan)
The Data Management Plan (DMP) folder contains all the files related to the management, handling, and protection of the data used in this research. These files ensure compliance with ethical, privacy, and legal requirements.

**Contents:**
- `DataScienceAI2_ReadMe_final.md`: A text file describing the contents of the DMP folder.


- `DataScienceAI2_codebook_final.md`: A codebook outlining and describing all variables used in data collection and analysis. 
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/e1de482a030fb3d647f702173b33e48074c03165/DMP/DataScienceAI2_codebook_final.md]

- `DataScienceAI2_Data_Management_Plan_final.pdf`: The Data Management Plan, based on the NWO template.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/598ab46c0e7a944b3e39df54c3e5656b9d901ad6/DMP/DataScienceAI2_Data_Management_Plan_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_BUas_Research_Ethics_review_Application_final.pdf`: The ethics review application, reviewed by staff but not submitted to the ethics board.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/c49522c752bc22c562777658b5901a12bca4ef77/DMP/DataScienceAI2_BUas_Research_Ethics_Review_Application_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_Research_Information_Letter_final.pdf`: A letter explaining the research and study participation details, to be provided to participants.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/5324e3b10a86c3d7ad7c7095210cfb5a0ba251c0/DMP/DataScienceAI2_Research_Information_Letter_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_Informed_Consent_Letter_final.pdf`: The consent form to be signed by participants after reviewing the research information letter.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/3d2625c10cac90e86fb2d3b077b57e62a2aca0c6/DMP/DataScienceAI2_Informed_Consent_Letter_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_Data_Storage_Protocol_final.pdf`: A document outlining procedures for storing and managing the collected data.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/3d2625c10cac90e86fb2d3b077b57e62a2aca0c6/DMP/DataScienceAI2_Data_Storage_Protocol_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_Privacy_and_GDPR_Checklist_final.pdf`: A checklist to ensure compliance with privacy and GDPR regulations.
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/65bea01f18792d7b6a560d5a3f501666cb87d8c6/DMP/DataScienceAI2_Privacy_and_GDPR_Checklist_final.pdf]

- `DataScienceAI2_FAIR_Checklist_final.pdf`: A checklist to ensure that the data follows the FAIR principles (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable).
[https://github.com/BredaUniversityADSAI/2024-25a-fai2-adsai-group-datascienceai-2-2/blob/3d2625c10cac90e86fb2d3b077b57e62a2aca0c6/DMP/DataScienceAI2_FAIR_Checklist_final.pdf]
